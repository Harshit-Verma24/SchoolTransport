About School Link Transport

Founded in 2009, we have been the trusted partner for thousands of families, providing safe, reliable, and comfortable school transportation services.

Our Journey

School Link Transport was founded in 2009 by Mandeep Singh with a simple mission: to provide safe and reliable transportation for school children. What started with just 5 buses has now grown into a fleet of 50+ modern buses serving over 5000 students across Delhi NCR.

Over the past 15 years, we have continuously evolved our services, incorporating the latest technology, safety measures, and comfort features. Our commitment to excellence has made us the preferred choice for parents and schools alike.
